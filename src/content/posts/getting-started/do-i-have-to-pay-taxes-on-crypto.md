# Do I Have to Pay Taxes on Crypto? A Beginner’s Reality Check

Taxes can feel scary, but understanding the basics reduces fear and increases confidence to start.

---

## 1. Buying vs Selling

- Buying crypto usually doesn’t trigger taxes  
- Selling or swapping may be taxable events

---

## 2. Trading vs Holding

- Short-term trading → potential capital gains  
- Long-term holding → usually deferred until sale

---

## 3. When Taxes Matter

- Small hobby trades may not matter immediately  
- Larger transactions or exchanges can require reporting

---

## 4. Beginner Takeaways

- Keep records of transactions  
- Use simple portfolio tracking ([Guide](/blog/getting-started/how-to-track-crypto-portfolio/))  
- Focus on learning and safety first
