# What Are Stablecoins and Why Beginners Should Care

Stablecoins are cryptocurrencies pegged to real-world assets like the USD. They provide stability and are essential for beginners navigating crypto.

---

## 1. Popular Stablecoins

- **USDC**: Transparent reserves, widely used  
- **USDT**: Popular but slightly riskier  
- Other algorithmic or crypto-backed options

---

## 2. Why Use Stablecoins

- Park funds without leaving crypto  
- Quick entry/exit from volatile coins  
- Participate in DeFi with minimal price risk

---

## 3. Yield Intro

- Earn small returns on stablecoins safely  
- Be aware of risk vs. reward  
- Avoid over-leveraging

---

## 4. On-Ramps and Off-Ramps

- Buying stablecoins on exchanges  
- Transferring to wallets safely  
- Integrate into memecoin strategies ([Tools](/tools))

---

## Beginner Takeaways

- Always double-check stablecoin type  
- Use them for planning purchases or trading timing  
- Foundation for safe DeFi and memecoin activity
