# How to Track Your Crypto Portfolio as a Beginner

Tracking your crypto holdings may seem complicated, but you can start simple and gradually increase sophistication.

---

## 1. Manual Tracking

- Spreadsheet with coins, purchase price, current value  
- Useful for privacy-conscious users  
- Learn wallet address basics ([Guide](/blog/getting-started/set-up-first-wallet/))

---

## 2. Automated Tools

- Free apps and wallets with tracking  
- Privacy trade-offs vs convenience  
- Great for memecoin launches to track multiple wallets ([Tools](/tools))

---

## 3. What Not to Obsess Over Early

- Avoid watching prices every minute  
- Focus on understanding holdings and allocations  
- Set simple check-ins weekly

---

## Beginner Takeaways

- Start manual if new  
- Add apps as comfort grows  
- Integrate with your first 30-day roadmap
