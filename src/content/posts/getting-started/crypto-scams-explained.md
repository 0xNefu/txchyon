# Crypto Scams Explained: How Beginners Actually Get Wiped

Crypto scams have evolved. Newbies often fall for phishing, fake airdrops, or approval drainers. Awareness is your first defense.

---

## 1. Phishing

- Emails or links pretending to be exchanges or wallets  
- Always check URLs and official sources

---

## 2. Fake Airdrops

- Scammers promise free tokens  
- Never approve random smart contracts  
- Use tools to verify legitimacy ([Tools](/tools))

---

## 3. Approval Drainers

- Malicious contracts drain wallets  
- Review approvals before signing

---

## 4. Social Engineering

- Telegram, Discord, X DMs  
- Impersonation of friends, influencers, or support staff

---

## Beginner Takeaways

- Awareness > panic  
- Start with small test amounts  
- Integrate safety practices early ([Guide](/blog/getting-started/what-to-do-before-you-buy-your-first-crypto/))
