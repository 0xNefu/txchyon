## What Is Time-Based and Threshold Rebalancing?

**Time-Based Rebalancing:** Adjust your portfolio at regular intervals (weekly, monthly, quarterly).  
**Threshold Rebalancing:** Adjust only when asset weights deviate by a certain percentage from target allocation.

---

## Advantages and Disadvantages

| Method | Advantages | Disadvantages |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| Time-Based | Predictable, easy to schedule | May rebalance unnecessarily |
| Threshold-Based | Efficient, reacts to real allocation drift | Requires monitoring, can miss small drifts |

## How to Choose a Method

- Consider trading fees  
- Consider portfolio size  
- Consider volatility of assets  
- Combine both for hybrid strategies

---

## Final Thoughts

Both methods can maintain portfolio balance.  
Choosing the right one depends on **cost, effort, and risk tolerance**.
