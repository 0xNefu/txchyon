## What Are Factor-Based Allocation Strategies?

Factor-based allocation assigns portfolio weights based on **measurable characteristics** (factors) such as:

- Momentum  
- Value  
- Volatility  
- Market capitalization  

This approach adds **systematic intelligence** to allocation decisions.

---

## Popular Factors for Crypto Portfolios

1. **Momentum:** Tilt toward assets with strong performance trends  
2. **Volatility:** Reduce exposure to highly volatile assets  
3. **Market Cap:** Weight according to liquidity and size  
4. **Correlation:** Avoid overconcentration in correlated assets

## Implementing Factor-Based Allocation

1. Select relevant factors  
2. Score each asset  
3. Assign weights based on factor scores  
4. Rebalance regularly according to factor thresholds  
5. Track performance using metrics from:  
**[How to Track Crypto Portfolio Performance (Metrics That Actually Matter)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

## Benefits & Risks

**Benefits:**
- Reduces emotional bias  
- Optimizes risk-adjusted returns  
- Adaptable across market cycles  

**Risks:**
- Overfitting factors  
- Ignoring transaction costs  
- Neglecting overall portfolio allocation

---

## Final Thoughts

Factor-based allocation adds **data-driven discipline** to your crypto portfolio. Combined with rebalancing and risk management, it can improve long-term outcomes.
