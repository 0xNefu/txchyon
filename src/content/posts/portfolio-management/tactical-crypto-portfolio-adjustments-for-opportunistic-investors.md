## What Are Tactical Portfolio Adjustments?

Tactical adjustments involve **short-term changes to your crypto allocation** in response to market signals:

- Momentum opportunities  
- Temporary risk-off moves  
- Event-driven adjustments  

> Builds on: **[Crypto Portfolio Allocation for Beginners (Simple, Low-Risk Framework)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Key Principles

1. Maintain core strategic allocation  
2. Limit tactical deviations to a small percentage  
3. Avoid emotional trading  
4. Track performance: **[How to Track Crypto Portfolio Performance (Metrics That Actually Matter)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**  
5. Consider risk: **[Crypto Portfolio Risk Management Explained (Protect Capital First)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Benefits

- Capture short-term opportunities  
- Improve risk-adjusted returns  
- Enhance flexibility without sacrificing strategy

---

## Risks

- Over-trading  
- Ignoring fees  
- Emotional decisions  
- Portfolio drift without proper rebalancing

---

## Final Thoughts

Tactical adjustments are **supplemental**, not a replacement for core allocation. When executed systematically, they can enhance performance while keeping risk controlled.
