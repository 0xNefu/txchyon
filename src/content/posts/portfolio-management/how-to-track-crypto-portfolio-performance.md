## Tools to Track Your Crypto Portfolio

- **Portfolio trackers:** Zerion, CoinStats, Delta  
- **Spreadsheets:** Google Sheets or Excel templates  
- **Automated dashboards:** Tools that pull exchange API data  
- **Custom scripts:** Python or JavaScript for advanced users

> Choose a method that matches your risk tolerance and complexity preference.

---

## Step-by-Step: Tracking Your Portfolio Performance

1. **Input Your Holdings**  
   Include all exchanges, wallets, and staking positions.

2. **Set Target Allocation**  
   Compare current weights vs allocation to track drift.

3. **Calculate Metrics**  
   Track total value, realized/unrealized gains, volatility.

4. **Visualize Data**  
   Charts make trends and drawdowns obvious.

5. **Review Regularly**  
   Weekly or monthly reviews are sufficient.
