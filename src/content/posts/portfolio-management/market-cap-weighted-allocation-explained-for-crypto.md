## What Is Market Cap Weighted Allocation?

Market cap weighting assigns portfolio percentages **proportional to each assetâ€™s market capitalization**:

- Bitcoin largest weight  
- Ethereum second  
- Smaller altcoins smaller weights

---

## Advantages

- Reflects market liquidity  
- Reduces risk of small illiquid altcoins dominating portfolio  
- Simple to implement  

> Ties into **[Crypto Portfolio Risk Management Explained (Protect Capital First)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Implementation

1. List all assets and their market caps  
2. Calculate total market cap of your portfolio assets  
3. Assign weights proportional to market cap  
4. Rebalance periodically  

> Links to performance tracking: **[How to Track Crypto Portfolio Performance (Metrics That Actually Matter)](https://txchyon.com/categories/portfolio-management/)**

---

## Limitations

- Large-cap dominance may reduce upside  
- Less flexible in capturing small-cap trends  
- May need adjustment for long-term strategic goals  

---

## Final Thoughts

Market cap weighting is a **stable, risk-conscious allocation method**, ideal for beginners or long-term investors who want to mirror market dominance.
